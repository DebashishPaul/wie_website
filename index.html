About IEEE WIE

IEEE North South University Student Branch, Women In Engineering ( WIE ) Affinity Group started their glorious journey from 20th June 2016. And from then it has been working and embracing the women who is following their dream of becoming an engineer and make this world a better place for the others.
WIE is always there trying to build up and sharpen the skills of the members and that is why it has been organizing several workshops and competitions. Events like Workshop on MATLab, Workshop on PCB, Penguin Mela, Communication Camp- A Boot Camp on Communication Skills Developmenthave been arranged and the response was tremendous.

IEEE NSU SB WIE aim to..

. Inspire female students to enhance their engineering career
. Organizing effective and impactful events
. Create leader among the women and give them components to build a successful career
. Improve IEEE Student – Teacher relationship
. Provide assistance and support to the student branch and section
